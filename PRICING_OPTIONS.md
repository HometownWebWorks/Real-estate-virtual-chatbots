# Real Estate Virtual Chatbots - Pricing Options

Last updated: April 27, 2026 9:46 PM -04:00

## Positioning

Real Estate Virtual Chatbots gives realtors a simple way to capture more buyer and seller leads without forcing prospects through a boring form. The chatbot asks the right questions, organizes the lead details, and helps the agent follow up faster.

The main value is not just "having a chatbot." The value is having a chatbot that is built around how a realtor actually works: buyer qualification, seller qualification, showing requests, home valuation interest, pre-approval needs, and clean follow-up details.

## Setup Fee

All plans include a one-time setup fee of **$50**.

This setup fee covers the initial configuration, basic realtor details, contact routing, chatbot setup, and first-version launch preparation.

## Starter Plan - $99/month

Best for solo agents who want a simple lead capture chatbot without a complicated setup.

Includes:

- Basic chatbot
- Lead capture through chat
- Buyer or seller intake flow
- Basic qualification questions
- FAQ answers
- Email alerts
- Direct chatbot link
- Basic mobile-friendly design

Recommended for:

- New agents
- Solo agents
- Agents who want a low-cost way to stop missing website or social media leads
- Realtors who want to test chatbot lead capture before adding deeper automation

## Pro Plan - $199/month

Best for agents who want a more complete buyer and seller lead system with stronger branding and organization.

Includes everything in Starter, plus:

- Custom branding
- Buyer and seller chatbot flows
- Google Sheets lead tracker
- Calendar booking link
- More tailored chatbot wording
- Better lead organization for follow-up
- Separate buyer and seller links

Recommended for:

- Active realtors
- Agents running ads or posting listings regularly
- Realtors who want separate buyer and seller lead capture links
- Agents who want leads organized automatically instead of scattered through texts, DMs, and emails

## Premium Plan - $299-$499/month

Best for higher-volume agents, teams, or realtors who want a more advanced lead automation system.

Includes everything in Pro, plus:

- CRM integration
- SMS alerts
- Listing-specific chatbot
- Monthly optimization
- More advanced qualification logic
- Custom FAQ tuning
- Performance review and updates
- More hands-on support

Recommended for:

- Busy agents
- Realtor teams
- Agents with multiple listings
- Agents running paid ads
- Realtors who want the chatbot to improve over time instead of staying static

Premium pricing depends on how much customization and integration work is needed.

## Why This Is Valuable Compared To Other Real Estate Chatbots

Many real estate chatbot products are either basic templates or larger platforms built for broad automation. Some tools are inexpensive but require the realtor to do more setup and customization themselves. Other real estate-focused tools can become more expensive as they add AI, CRM features, custom branding, or multi-channel automation.

This offer is built to sit in the sweet spot:

- Affordable monthly pricing
- Realtor-specific buyer and seller flows
- Simple setup fee
- Clear local-business service instead of a generic software-only experience
- Customization without enterprise pricing
- Practical lead capture that focuses on what realtors actually need to know

The goal is to give agents a useful, professional chatbot that feels personalized without making them pay for a large CRM platform or figure out a complicated chatbot builder by themselves.

## Market Reference Notes

Current market examples show that real estate chatbot pricing can vary widely. Some template-style products are cheaper but usually require more self-setup. More advanced real estate chatbot platforms commonly charge higher monthly prices when features like AI responses, branding, CRM integration, appointment booking, and multi-channel automation are included.

Useful references:

- Realty AI lists a Standard plan around $100/month and a Premium plan around $250/month with features such as booking, custom branding, and CRM integration.
- Luxury Presence notes that real estate chatbot pricing can range from free or low-cost basic plans to paid tiers above $400/month, depending on AI features, integrations, channels, and usage.
- TechRadar's Chime/Lofty review notes real estate CRM pricing tiers around $499 to $1500/month, showing how quickly broader real estate lead platforms can become expensive.
